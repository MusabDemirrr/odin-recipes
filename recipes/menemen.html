Menemen


A traditional Turkish breakfast dish made with eggs, tomatoes, peppers, and spices,
cooked together for a soft and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 small onion (optional), chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© onion (if using) and green pepper until soft.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  3. Crack eggs over the mixture and stir gently.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook until eggs are just set, serve hot with bread.
