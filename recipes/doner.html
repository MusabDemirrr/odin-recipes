Döner


A delicious Turkish dish made of seasoned meat, cooked on a vertical rotisserie,
and often served with bread, vegetables, and sauces.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken or beef
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Bread or pita
  • Vegetables (tomato, lettuce, cucumber)

Steps

  1. Slice the meat thinly and marinate with spices
  2. Wash and chop the vegetables (onion, tomato, lettuce, cucumber).
  3. Cook the meat on a pan or grill.
  4. Warm the flatbread or pita, place the meat and vegetables inside.
  5. Add sauce if desired and serve.
