Lahmacun


A thin Turkish flatbread topped with a flavorful mixture of minced meat
,vegetables, and spices, baked until crispy.

Ingredients

  • 250g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Flatbread dough

Steps

  1. Mix ground meat with onion, tomato, green pepper, tomato paste, and spices.
  2. Roll out flatbread dough thinly.
  3. Spread the meat mixture evenly over the dough.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C (425°F) for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, optionally with parsley and lemon.
